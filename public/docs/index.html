Remix/Miner
To use this site as your own miner for MintMe you must remix the project.
Once you have remixed the project you need to edit the /miner page
by replacing the current script with your own from CoinIMP.

Steps

  • Remix The project On Glitch


  • Create A CoinIMP Account


  • Access your "Dashboard" on CoinIMP and "GET THE CODE" ; Paste code In
    /miner/index.html


    * It can take up to 48 hours before you see a change in your dashboard.
  • Add your MintMe Wallet
    • To send MintMe go to the "Wallet" page on MintMe and copy the deposit string for your wallet.
    You can add it under "Cash Out" on CoinImP.

  • Trade/Swap
    • Once you completed your miner and have mined MintMe you can trade it for MTCG, ETH, BNB, BTC, or USDC on Mintme.
