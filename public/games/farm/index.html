How To Play

Objective:
Your goal is to collect laid eggs and crack them! Each egg has Yolks inside, when you crack them you receive the stored Yolks

Laying Eggs:
Laid eggs are created by your magic chicken, just click it. Your magic chicken follows you as long as you have feed.

Cracking Eggs:
You can crack eggs in any location, as long as you have enough energy. If you fully deplete your energy you will have to fully recharge before you’re able to crack eggs again.

Energy:
You can recharge your energy by clicking the sleep button while on your farm, when traveling you can rent a bed if you run out.

Chicken Feed:
Your chicken won’t come back to you if it’s hungry and you have no feed, make sure you have feed for your chicken to follow you around, you can get feed in the farmers market.
